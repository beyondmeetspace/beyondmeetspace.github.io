Special issues.

Beyond Meet Space members serve as guest editors for two special issues on metaverse-mediated communication and meaningful XR experiences, showcasing theoretically grounded, empirically rigorous scholarship from across the XR research community.

Published · 2025

Journal of Media Psychology

Metaverse-Mediated Communication: A Call for Theory-Driven XR Research

This special issue, made in collaboration with the Meaningful XR 2024 conference, offers theory-driven examinations of how we interact in and with metaverse and immersive environments. The scholars in this issue represent novel approaches to theorizing and examining metaverse interaction, including advances in augmented-reality theorization and extensions of existing theories about augmented metaverse contexts.

Guest Editors: David Beyea, Vivian Hsueh Hua Chen, Rabindra Ratan, Maxwell Foxman, Alex P. Leith, Brian Klebig.

About our editorial practice

The Meaningful XR pipeline.

The Meaningful XR conference is the engine for both special issues. Selected papers from each conference cycle receive invitations to develop their work for journal publication, building a sustained community of researchers focused on virtual meetings, XR-mediated interaction, and the future of collaborative technology.

Interested in contributing to future calls? Watch the Meaningful XR conference cycle for upcoming submission opportunities.

Pipeline at a glance

Step 1. Submit to the annual Meaningful XR conference call.

Step 2. Selected papers receive invitations to expand for a special-issue submission.

Step 3. Invited papers go through standard journal peer review under the guest-editor team.

Step 4. Accepted papers anchor a curated theme issue, published via the host journal.
