Three findings that frame the work.

Our contribution across a five-study research program on virtual meetings, well-being, and equity in synchronous mediated interaction.

Finding 01Meta-analysis

Feeling trapped drives fatigue.

.33r

A meta-analysis of 38 studies identified "feeling trapped" as the strongest predictor of videoconferencing fatigue, outpacing audio issues, camera problems, and meeting length.

Beyea et al., 2025 →
Finding 02Survey · N=812

Avatar customization is an equity tool.

+18%Δ

Women and people of color place significantly higher value on avatar customization in virtual workplaces — a measurable equity signal that reframes customization as infrastructure.

Lim et al., 2024 →
Finding 03In-world study

Play reduces fatigue.

64cap

Playful design — gesture tools, stylized environments, ambient virtual objects — reduces fatigue and increases engagement, shifting virtual meetings from endurance to participation.

DiGRA 2024 →
