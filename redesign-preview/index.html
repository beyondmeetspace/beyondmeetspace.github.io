Three directions for the BMS site redesign.

We're refreshing beyondmeet.space to read clearly as a research collective chasing multi-institution grants — and to look distinct from other lab/group sites in our orbit. Below are three palette + typography directions on the same content. Pick a favorite, or tell us what's missing.

Constraints: must signal "serious research collective" to NSF program officers and partner PIs; must visibly differentiate from a typical AI-startup aesthetic; must not look like a single-PI lab page. All three options share the same layout, content, and structure — only color and typography vary.

Option A

Warm cream + oxblood

Fraunces serif on warm paper. Reads Quanta Magazine / longform-journalism. The institutional move.

How to give feedback

  1. Open each option. There's a switcher in the top-right of every page to flip between them.
  2. Reply on the Discord thread (or wherever Alex shared this) with: A, B, or C — plus a sentence on what worked or didn't.
  3. If none of these land, say so. Hybrids and "this part of X with that part of Y" are welcome.
